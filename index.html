ILITE Robotics Worlds 2019

ILITE Robotics is Battlefield High School's First Robotics Competition Team that I have been apart of for the past 3 years. The video to the left shows my experience at the World Championships in 2019 at Detroit, Michigan. Click on the image to go watch the video!

This was my last robotics competition and the video was made to encapsulate the start to the end of my senior year. I want to personally thank everyone on ILITE Robotics for giving such a wonderful experience throughout my high school career and I will cherish every moment with the team as time winds down to zero.
